5、更改IP

centos ip更改

默认值：

DEVICE="eth0"
BOOTPROTO="dhcp"
DHCP_HOSTNAME="quickstart.cloudera"
HOSTNAME="quickstart.cloudera"
HWADDR="00:0C:29:D3:4D:5C"
IPV6INIT="no"
MTU="1500"
NM_CONTROLLED="yes"
ONBOOT="yes"
TYPE="Ethernet"
UUID="f127faf3-7a1f-4bca-a404-0119665a8459"

注意：使用sudo

 sudo vim /etc/sysconfig/network-scripts/ifcfg-eth0

修改为:

DEVICE="eth0"
BOOTPROTO="static"
DHCP_HOSTNAME="quickstart.cloudera"
HOSTNAME="quickstart.cloudera"
HWADDR="00:0C:29:D3:4D:5C"
IPV6INIT="no"
MTU="1500"
NM_CONTROLLED="yes"
ONBOOT="yes"
TYPE="Ethernet"
UUID="f127faf3-7a1f-4bca-a404-0119665a8459"
IPADDR=192.168.6.6
NETMASK=255.255.255.0

重新启动即可:

sudo service network restart
